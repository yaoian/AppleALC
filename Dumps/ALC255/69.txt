Codec dump for Acer Aspire 3 A315-56-327T

This patch is used for enabling the headset microphone: https://github.com/torvalds/linux/blob/d07f6ca923ea0927a1024dfccafc5b53b61cfecc/sound/pci/hda/patch_realtek.c#L6976

Headset mic won't work out of the box, you need to send appropriate verbs in order to use them. You can use:
ComboJack: "https://github.com/hackintosh-stuff/ComboJack",
ALCPlugFix-Swift: "https://github.com/black-dragon74/ALCPlugFix-Swift",
or "alc-verb".
You can find the verbs here: https://github.com/hackintosh-stuff/ComboJack/blob/master/ComboJack_Source/Combo%20Jack/hda-verb.m
